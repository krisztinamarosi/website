What We Do

DIGITAL BIOMARKERS HEALTH TRACKING A.I. COACHING

Applying cutting-edge research to wearable devices

Our science team identifies and implements the latest "digital biomarkers" derived from wearable devices. All digital biomarkers are backed by peer-reviewed research.

Data visualization to evaluate progress.

Key to lasting behavioral change is tracking one's progress over time. Our data science team is dedicated to delivering effective visual graphics for extra motivation.

Personalized goal-setting powered by artificial intelligence (A.I.)

Your body's needs are unique. Cookie-cutter exercise regimens are not a one-size fits all. We use machine learning and A.I. to assess your fitness levels and adjust your fitness goals accordingly in real-time.

Developing Digital Biomarkers of Health Using Real-World Wearable Data
